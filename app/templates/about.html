About CSPB Course Reviews

Welcome to CSPB Course Reviews, a platform designed to help students share their experiences and insights about courses offered in the Computer Science Post-Baccalaureate program at the University of Colorado Boulder. Our goal is to provide a space where students can make informed decisions about their course selections based on the feedback and reviews from their peers.

Team Infinity is dedicated to creating a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for students to access and share course reviews. We believe that informed decision-making is crucial for academic success, and our platform aims to facilitate this process by providing a reliable source of information about courses in the CSPB program. Sign up or log in to contribute your own reviews and help fellow students navigate their academic journey.

CSPB 3308: Software Development Project

This project is part of CSPB 3308: Software Development Methods and Tools at CU Boulder. The course focuses on developing software solutions in a team environment, emphasizing collaboration, communication, and practical application of software development principles. Team Infinity has worked together to design and implement this course review platform, showcasing our skills in web development, database management, and user experience design. After the completion of this course, we hope to continue improving and expanding the platform.
